ACCESSIBILITY

We want everyone to enjoy VR gaming. Here are the features we've built to make that happen.

Our Commitment

Pulse VR is committed to making our website and venues accessible to everyone. We follow WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines.

Accessibility team email: accessibility@pulsevr.co.uk

Phone (Text relay available): 020 1234 5678

Visual Accessibility

👁️

Adjustable Text Size

Use the buttons above to make text bigger or smaller on all pages.

🎨

High Contrast Mode

Switch to high contrast black and white for better visibility.

📝

Alt Text on Images

Every image has a description for screen readers.

🔍

Screen Reader Compatible

Works with NVDA, VoiceOver and other screen readers.

Hearing Accessibility

🔊

Closed Captions

All videos have captions. Venue briefings have subtitles.

👁️

Visual Notifications

Important alerts show on screen as well as making sound.

💬

Text Support

Email or live chat available for text communication.

🔄

Induction Loops

All venues have hearing induction loops at reception.

Motor Accessibility

⌨️

Keyboard Navigation

Use Tab key to move through links and buttons. No mouse needed.

👆

Large Buttons

All buttons are big enough to tap easily on phones and tablets.

⏱️

No Time Limits

Take as long as you need to fill in forms. Nothing expires.

♿

Wheelchair Access

All venues have ramps, wide doors and accessible VR booths.

Cognitive Accessibility

📖

Simple Language

We write in plain English. No complicated words or jargon.

📐

Consistent Layout

Every page has the same structure. Navigation is always in the same place.

✅

Clear Instructions

Forms tell you what to do. Error messages explain how to fix problems.

🖼️

Icons with Text

Icons always have text labels so you know what they mean.

WCAG 2.1 Compliance

We aim to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards across all pages.

Perceivable - Text alternatives, captions, adaptable content
Operable - Keyboard accessible, enough time, no seizures
Understandable - Readable text, predictable layout, error help
Robust - Works with assistive technologies
